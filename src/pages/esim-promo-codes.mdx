### TooSim — thinnest promo game

<a href={T.affiliate_url} rel="sponsored nofollow noopener" target="_blank">TooSim</a> has no permanent new-user code. It occasionally publishes 15–20% offers through its blog and social channels, and its country pages advertise "promo codes available." Worth a quick check before buying, but don't expect a standing discount — and given TooSim's pricing in some markets, a 15% code may still leave it more expensive than rivals' list prices.

## FAQ

**Which provider is cheapest after discounts?**
Depends on destination. Pair this guide with our [full pricing comparison](/travel-esim-comparison/) — a promo code on an overpriced plan can still cost more than a rival's list price.

**Do these codes work in the app and on the website?**
Usually both. Enter the code at checkout before payment; the discount applies instantly. Codes are for new accounts only and can't be stacked.
