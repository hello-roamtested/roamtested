## FAQ

**Do these VPNs work in mainland China?**
ExpressVPN, Surfshark, and Astrill test most reliably in 2026; NordVPN works via obfuscated servers; PureVPN is hit-or-miss. Install and log in **before** you arrive — provider websites are blocked inside China.

**Can I get a truly free VPN for China?**
Effectively no — independent tests show free tiers rarely survive current firewall upgrades. The 30-day money-back guarantees above are the practical "free month."

**Do I still need a VPN if I have a travel eSIM?**
For eSIM data, no — roaming data routes outside the firewall. But hotel Wi-Fi, laptops, and any local network still need a VPN. Most travelers to China want both; see our [eSIM comparison for China](/best-esim-for-china/).

**Are prices different if I buy from inside China?**
Prices are the same, but payment pages may not load. Buy and install before departure.
